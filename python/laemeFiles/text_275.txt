{=The following text consists of Scribe B\'s marginal and interlinear\n
corrections to the text of Ancrene Riwle written by Scribe A. In most\n
cases these corrections bring Scribe A\'s original text, version C,\n
into line with the revised text found in Cambridge, Corpus Christi\n
College MS 402 (version A, Ancrene Wisse). For this and other reasons,\n
Dobson argues (1972: xciii-cxl, esp. xcvi) that Scribe B of BL Cotton\n
Cleopatra C.vi was the author of Ancrene Riwle/Wisse, whom he further\n
tentatively identifies with "Brianus de Lingan Canonicus secularis\n
monesterii de wigmore" via interpretation of the anagram at the end\n
of the text (Dobson 1976: 312-68). Bella Millet (1992) has thrown\n
doubt on the detail of Dobson\'s conclusions and she argues strongly for\n
a Dominican author for Ancrene Riwle/Wisse. The Dominican house that\n
would most closely fit with the linguistic facts is Shrewsbury. It is\n
true that a Dominican Friar based in Shrewsbury at the time of writing\n
Ancrene Riwle would not need to have originated from there, but\n
linguistically there is nothing to prevent Scribe B\'s language from\n
being fitted there. If one accepts the placings of the language of the\n
Titus Ancrene Riwle and of the Titus Wooing of our Lord, the forms in\n
those texts give a more northerly confirmation of some of Scribe B\'s\n
forms at this early date. The somewhat contrasting Shrewsbury\n
material in LALME is from 200 years later. The details of the positioning of\n
Scribe B\'s corrections and revisions within the main text may be found\n
in Dobson (1972) and are not repeated here. I have not attempted to\n
transcribe bits of words added or substituted by Scribe B, only whole\n
words and longer stretches of text. These are given the grammatical\n
tags suitable to their position in the surrounding text, which may\n
be found in Dobson (1972). Although the text here is thus\n
discontinuous, line breaks and folio references are indicated in the\n
usual way. Braces with no back slash indicate textual discontinuity\n
but no line break=}\n
